Khitab-e-Swar Singing Competition Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age categories: 8-17 years (Junior) and 18-30 years (Senior)
  • Individual participation only (no groups/duets)
  • Valid government-issued ID required for age verification
  • Professional singers under record contracts ineligible

3. Competition Structure

  1. Auditions: 3-minute performances at designated centers
  2. Semi-Finals: Shortlisted candidates perform original compositions
  3. Grand Finale: Live performance with professional accompaniment

4. Performance Rules

  • Maximum duration: 3 minutes (auditions), 5 minutes (finale)
  • Allowed formats: Karaoke tracks (USB/CD) or acapella
  • Prohibited content: Explicit/vulgar lyrics or gestures
